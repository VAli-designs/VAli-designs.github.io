UK Florist UX Research Study

User tests in France

Market : Europe mainly UK, Germany and France
Service : Online Florist service

STUDY

  • Explore, discover and understand the relationship of the users with the brand.
  • Map the customer's needs, competitor services,
  • Analyse digital (website, eshop) and physical (packaging and product concept) performance.

GOAL

  • Help to increase the online conversion rate
  • Help to take communication's decision by having a better understanding of user's brand image perception
  • Adapt the brand's concept to the french customer's needs
  • Improve the website/eshop thanks to Usability test

THE CLIENT'S NEED / VERBATIM

"We would like to assess the strenghts/Blockages of our site in terms of UX (but also more generally in terms of product feeling, checkout flow..."

All About the process - 4 steps

1/4
Beginning of the study

First meetings were at the phone, skype and we were lucky to have the opportunity to met in Paris.
- We mainly discussed about: - The story behind the brand - the next steps in the brand development - the previous UX research studies that had been done

2/4
User test preparation

Around 10 days before the user test sessions:
- we wrote the test protocol in collaboration with the team's project - we started the recruitment of the targeted audience - we booked a laboratory in Paris as 2 of the team members will assist at the sessions

3/4
User test sessions

A Senior UX Researcher moderate the 2 days of User Test in individual sessions.

The User Test has been on mobile and desktop eshop.
The sessions has been recorded: screen + user's face.
The room has been recorded with the full interview, packaging, discovery of the product sessions.

4/4
The analysis, Final results

The Expert in UX Research wrote a synthesis with problem, description, recommandation pages. Difficulties have been prioritized according to user's pain,recommandations have been given through quick-wins as much as possible, best practices has been highlighted. The whole research has been synthesized in a ppt file.
