Car dealership UX Research Study

4 days UX Research on site in France

Market : German brand, Europe
Service : Brand Car Seller

TO SUM UP

The study is about to understand, explore, discover Customer's need in a buying process of a car and especially in the Dealership service. How to provide a new digital Showroom Experience to customers ?


GOAL

Increasing the conversion rate of the visitors, up-sell rate of product

Providing a User Experience adapted to the customer's journey to increase the satisfaction in the brand and in the product

Understand the specificity in each countries as we managed a study in France, Germany and in the UK.

THE CLIENT'S NEED

"Create a new retail experience for our customers that spans both the digital and physical to deliver a seamless customer journey online and in dealership"

All About the process - 4 steps

1/4
Beginning of the study

We mainly discussed about: - the next steps in the brand development - the previous User Experience Studies - The reasons for launching this study

2/4
UX Research Preparation

We worked with the team project on a test guide which has been translated for the 3 countries:

  • customer's interview
  • we started the recruitment of the targeted audience
  • dealer's staff interview

3/4
The Sessions

A Senior UX Researcher observed and interviewed around 15 people in the dealership, during 4 days on site in France.

4/4
The analysis, Final results

The Expert in UX Research wrote a synthesis with problem, description, recommandation pages. Difficulties have been prioritized according to user's pain, recommandations have been given through quick-wins as much as possible, best practices has been highlighted.

The whole research has been synthesized in a ppt file and presented via Skype to the international team.
